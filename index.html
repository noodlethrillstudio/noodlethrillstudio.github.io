WILL CLARKE

ANIMATOR AND ARTIST

Will is an artist who usually lives in Bristol, UK. He works in animation, utilising hand drawn, CG, mixed media and experimental workflows and he's been known to dabble in a bit of creative coding and scripting, too. His films have been selected in international festivals including Annecy, Monstra, Encounters, and Anifilm.
